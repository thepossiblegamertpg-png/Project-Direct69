Project 1 : Project DIRECT


   FIVE SECTION THOUGHT CATEGORIZATION FRAME-WORK

Purpose : To gamify organization of tasks, and collect them in a prioritized fashion. The scoreboard correlates to the priority of the task completed, so a higher rated priority task will naturally yield a higher score. This adds a layer of dopamine release to completion, and may very well serve as a greater framework for project direct.

Utility : Very general and serves as a framework. Incredibly accessible.
